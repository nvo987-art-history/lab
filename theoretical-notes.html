Conceptual Direction

The theoretical framework of the research is based on the understanding of color as a generative and relational phenomenon rather than a fixed attribute.

This approach considers perception as a temporal process in which visual phenomena emerge through interaction, memory, and spatial relations.

In contrast, AI-generated imagery is approached as a system in which visual output is produced instantaneously through computational processes, without temporal unfolding.

The purpose of this section is to establish a precise conceptual language for describing these different modes of formation.
