Theoretical Notes

This section contains the conceptual and theoretical layer of the NV0987 Lab research environment. It is intended as a space for the articulation of key ideas, definitions, and emerging theoretical structures.

Unlike research notes, which document thinking in progress, this section focuses on the clarification and stabilization of concepts that form the basis of the research framework.

Conceptual Domains

  • Relational color systems
  • Perceptual development
  • Temporal structures of perception
  • Memory and color
  • AI image generation models
  • Semantic and conceptual frameworks

Function

Theoretical notes serve as an intermediate layer between exploratory research and formal publication. They define terminology, establish conceptual relations, and provide a structured framework for ongoing analysis.

This space supports the transition from fragmented observations toward coherent theoretical models.

Status

This section is under continuous development. Theoretical structures are progressively refined as the research evolves.

Content published here represents intermediate stages of conceptual clarification and may be subject to revision.
