Research Domains

The laboratory focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence, semantic web technologies, and knowledge graph systems in order to develop research environments where data can be interpreted by both humans and autonomous software agents.

These domains form the conceptual backbone of the NV0987 research network. Each research direction contributes to the broader goal of building an interoperable knowledge infrastructure connected through the decentralized identity authority did:web:identity.nvo987.us.

  • Verifiable Credentials — decentralized verification mechanisms for research artifacts
