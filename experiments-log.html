Experiments Log

This section documents the experimental dimension of the NV0987 Lab research environment. It records practical investigations, tests, and iterative explorations related to color perception and AI-generated imagery.

Unlike theoretical or analytical sections, the experiments log focuses on process, execution, and observable outcomes rather than conceptual abstraction.

Experimental Focus

  • AI image generation experiments and prompt-based variations
  • Exploration of color behavior in generated visual systems
  • Comparative tests between human perception and algorithmic output
  • Material and perceptual experiments related to relational color
  • Testing of visual structures across different tools and models

Method

Experiments are conducted as iterative processes, where each test builds upon previous observations. The goal is not immediate validation, but the gradual emergence of patterns and structural insights.

Each entry may include:

  • Experiment description and objective
  • Tools or systems used (AI models, software, environments)
  • Process and parameters
  • Observed results and variations
  • Preliminary interpretation or questions

Status

This log is continuously updated as new experiments are conducted. Entries may vary in level of detail and are not necessarily finalized.

The material presented here serves as a bridge between practical experimentation and later analytical or theoretical development.
