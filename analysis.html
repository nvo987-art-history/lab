Analysis Layer

This section represents the analytical layer of the NV0987 Lab research environment. It is designed to host structured interpretations, comparative studies, and process-based evaluations emerging from ongoing research.

At the current stage, the analysis layer is under active development. Content will be published progressively as the research dataset expands.

Methodological Framework

The analytical process is based on the comparison of two distinct systems: human perceptual color formation and AI-generated image structures.

  • Relational color systems in human perception
  • Temporal development of visual experience
  • Memory and perceptual continuity
  • AI image generation as computational output
  • Structural differences between process-based and instant systems

The goal is not evaluation, but structural understanding of how these systems operate.

Planned Analysis Modules

  • Comparative perceptual analysis
  • Temporal vs instantaneous image formation
  • Color as a generative system
  • AI image output structure analysis
  • Memory and color relation mapping
